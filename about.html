About Us

Connecting Timorese students in Romania through education, culture, and community

Who We Are

The Association of Timorese Students in Romania (AETLR) is a student-led organization dedicated to supporting Timorese students pursuing higher education in Romania. We foster academic excellence, cultural preservation, and a strong community spirit.

🎯 Our Mission

To create a supportive environment where Timorese students can excel academically while maintaining their cultural identity.

👁️ Our Vision

To be the leading platform for Timorese students in Romania, promoting excellence in education and cultural preservation.

💡 Our Values

  • ✓ Unity and Community Support
  • ✓ Academic Excellence
  • ✓ Cultural Preservation
  • ✓ Leadership Development
  • ✓ International Cooperation
