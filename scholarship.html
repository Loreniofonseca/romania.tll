🎓 Romanian Government Scholarship

The Romanian Government offers fully-funded scholarships for international students from non-EU countries through the official Study in Romania platform.

What Does the Scholarship Offer?

  • Full tuition fee coverage
  • Monthly scholarship allowance
  • Free accommodation in university dormitories
  • Medical insurance
  • Romanian language preparatory year
